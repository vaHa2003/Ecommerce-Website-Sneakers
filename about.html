About Solstice Scribes

OPULENT WRITING INSTRUMENTS HANDCRAFTED BY Solstice Scribes

Solstice Scribes pens are individually handcrafted to exacting standards, yet with subtle differences to ensure that each pen is truly unique.
We think you will agree that your Solstice Scribes is the best luxury pen on the market today and in a class all by itself.

Solstice Scribes makes the finest writing instruments for discerning top performing executives and sales professionals. Sophisticated and exquisite,
bold and distinguished, Solstice Scribes Pens are the embodiment of professional success and are the prized possessions of their owners.

Solstice Scribes was born from a passion for the art of handwriting and a desire to create pens that are not only tools but also symbols of elegance and personalization. Founded by individuals who understand the value of this art, Solstice Scribes is committed to delivering a unique and luxurious writing experience.

Solstice Scribes began with a simple yet meaningful story, inspired by a treasured heirloom: an old pen belonging to the founder, a companion that witnessed each significant milestone of his life. Through faded pages, that pen carved out memories, dreams, and promises. With the belief that a pen is more than just a tool but a symbol of life’s stories, Solstice Scribes was created to craft writing instruments that can accompany our customers on their own journeys.
We believe that each Solstice Scribes pen carries the mission to connect, to cherish every handwritten word, every feeling, and each personal imprint. From the dedicated hands of artisans to every meticulously finished detail, we deliver not only a product but a part of a timeless spirit—a tool to capture life’s precious stories.

Vision: To become a symbol of sophistication and class in the world of signature pens, offering timeless value to those who appreciate perfection.

Mission: Solstice Scribes is dedicated to designing and crafting luxury pens that satisfy even the most discerning customers and elevate the art of handwriting to a new level.

The name "Solstice Scribes" is inspired by the celestial phenomenon of the solstice—a moment in the year when light reaches its peak or its quietest point. It symbolizes balance and power, and represents the refinement in each stroke of writing and the elegance of every product we offer.

Solstice Scribes caters to discerning and sophisticated individuals who view signature pens not just as tools but as essential elements for expressing personal style and values. Our clients are people with a refined taste, seeking exquisitely crafted products.

Today, Solstice Scribes is present in high-end markets around the world. Our products are available in prestigious pen boutiques and premier retail locations, offering easy access for those who seek refined, luxurious writing instruments.

“Solstice Scribes was born from a passion for the elegance and artistry of signature pens. We aim to create pens that are not just tools but symbols of appreciation and personal style.”
