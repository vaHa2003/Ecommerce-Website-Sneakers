Common FAQs

Are your pens handmade?

Yes. Each Solstice Scribes undergoes a painstaking creation process involving 48 steps that transform raw materials into masterpieces of elegance and functionality. The cap and barrel are handmade and hand-turned on a lathe. The metal portions are created through a manufacturing process due to the rare metals used. Proudly made in the USA.

How can I know my Solstice Scribes are authentic?

To have your pen authenticated, you would need to mail it to us for inspection and authentication. We offer an evaluation of originality for all our pens. Contact us at solsticesribes@gmail.com for more information about the process.

We are sorry, but we are unable to process exchanges. You must return the pen under the Return Policy Procedure and then purchase a new one.
